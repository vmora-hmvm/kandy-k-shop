Questions?

FAQ

How fresh are the candies?

Every order is packed fresh to order. We make small batches daily to ensure maximum flavor and quality. Your candy ships within 24-48 hours of making.

What's the shelf life?

Our chamoy candy treats stay fresh for 2-3 weeks at room temperature. For best results, keep sealed and away from direct heat.

Do you offer local pickup?

Yes! We offer local pickup in the Los Angeles area. Select "Local Pickup" at checkout and we'll coordinate a time that works for you.

Can I customize my order?

Absolutely! Mix and match any flavors in any size. Just let us know your preferences in the order notes and we'll make it happen.

Do you do events and parties?

Yes! We offer party trays and bulk orders for events. DM us on Instagram @kandykshop for custom quotes and availability.

How do I pay?

We accept Venmo, Zelle, and card payments via Stripe. Pick whichever's easiest for you at checkout!
